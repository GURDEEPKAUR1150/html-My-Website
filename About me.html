About Me: Gurdeep Kaur - From Banking to Coding Enthusiast

Hello, I'm Gurdeep Kaur, and I'd like to share my journey from a seasoned banker to an aspiring coder. My professional voyage has been nothing short of a remarkable adventure, filled with growth, challenges, and learning experiences.

A Banking Odyssey

I embarked on my professional journey with the State Bank of India in 1994, serving as a humble clerk-cum-cashier. Over the years, I navigated the dynamic landscape of banking, climbing the ladder through numerous promotions, and ultimately attaining the prestigious position of Chief Manager. This incredible journey allowed me to accumulate extensive experience in managing branches, where I oversaw a substantial business portfolio, valued at around 250 crores, for an impressive eight years.

My educational journey was equally diverse, having earned a Bachelor of Commerce degree from Delhi University and a Bachelor of Education degree from Annamalai University. Initially, my career path led me to the world of education, where I spent two fulfilling years as a teacher before transitioning into the realm of banking.
